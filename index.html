Manage Mediction

At U&DOC, we believe that taking your medications as prescribed is a crucial part of
maintaining your health and well-being. Whether you're starting a new medication, have
questions about your current prescriptions, or simply want to
stay organized, we're here to support you every step of the way.


Key Features:


1. Medication List: Maintain an up-to-date list of all your medications, including prescription
drugs, over-the-counter products, and supplements.


2. Medication Reminders: Never miss a dose again with our medication reminder feature. Set
customizable reminders for each medication, including dosage instructions and timing, so you
can stay on track with your treatment plan.


3. Refill Requests: Request prescription refills directly through our platform. We'll send your
request to your healthcare provider, making it convenient and hassle-free to maintain your
medication supply.
